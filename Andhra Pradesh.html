About the brand

Andhra Pradesh is a state of India, located in the southeastern part of the subcontinent. The state draws its name from the Andhra people, who have inhabited the area since antiquity and developed their own language, Telugu. The Andhras’ contribution to India’s cultural heritage is substantial. Architecture and painting have been highly developed arts in the region since ancient times. The kuchipudi style of dance is unique in the Indian tradition, while Karnatak (South Indian) music has derived much from Andhra roots.
