Product

This alphabet game set is an educational tool that serves to improve children's visual analytical skills and concept understanding.The goal of this teaching material is for children to identify alphabets and gain better readability. It will help improve their analytical skills through understanding concept of alphabet anatomy.

The product consists of 3 decks of cards, each having alphabets illustrated in different styles. Kids have to sort the deck matching alphabets from each style. They do this based on their understanding of the alphabet anatomy and ability of visual analysis.
