How will giraffe save the world!

Giraffes being the tallest and one of the kindest animals on earth, are a worthy topic for some creative imagination. How can they help with global warming and its consequences?


Inspired by the Chipko movement in India, Giraffes will stand-up against the felling of trees, wiring up their necks to the trees.





When the water level rises beyond control, life will evovle accordingly. Giraffes will be able to feed other animals on their backs through the evolved patched windows on its skin.
