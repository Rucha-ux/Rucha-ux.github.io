Asopalav Plant

The trees of Asopalav are considered sacred and magical in the Hindu culture due to its uncountable uses and advantages. I have imaged 2 scenarios from its advantages.


    Asopalav leaves help to prevent insects in the
    neighbourhood by attracting them towards itself.
    It also promotes positivity in the surroundings.
