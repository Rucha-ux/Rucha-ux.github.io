Childhood Stories

As kids, we all have many fantasies, fears, dreams, mysteries and cooked-up stories. Here, I have illustrated 2 stories that I used to believe.


I always asked my mother how people on other planet would be living. I imagined that they did have constructions all around just like us. I also felt that they were watching us with big tubular binoculars.





As kids, adults played with our mind by telling us that if we swallow a watermelon seed, the plant will grow inside our belly. I went ahead to imagine how that would look like.
