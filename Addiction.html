Problem Statement

Phone addiction has become a global concern. Researchers, scientists, teachers and many others are worried about excessive addiction to smart phones, its games and apps. Companies are taking up responsibility on how to control this.

Here, I have designed a few solutions to 2 of the most addictive apps.




User Research

I analyzed various aspects that play a role in increasing phone time. The research was to find out how users reacted to/ what they thought about their addiction. User rearch helped me figure out the tone of my solutions.
The research was conducted through an online survey and personal interviews with friends and family members.
